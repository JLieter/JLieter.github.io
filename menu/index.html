Stew

Prep Time:
 15 Minutes
Total Time:
 3 Hours
Oven Temp:
 Pressure Cooker
Servings:
 6-8

Ingredients:

3lbs Rump Roast 6 Russet Potatoes 6 Carrots 6 Cubes Beef Boullion
4 cups Bisquick box mix 1 1/3 cups milk

Directions:

  • Start with Rump Roast in Pressure cooker.
  • Fill with water until 3/4 meat is covered.
  • Add 6 cubes Beef Boullion or to taste.
  • Start Pressure cooker on high for one hour.
  • Peel and slice carrots, peel and cube potatoes.
  • After pressure cooker finishes, allow to slow release steam.
  • Add carrots and potatoes, and cook on high for 20 minutes.
  • Prepare dumplings by adding four cups Bisquick box mixing and 1 1/3 cups milk to a mixing bowl. Mix thoroughly.
  • When pressure cooker finishes, allow to slow release and remove meat.
  • Set roast to the side and cover.
  • Scoop the mixture by spoonful into the pot to create dumpling size and shape.
  • Cook on high for 10 more minutes.
  • Allow to cool.
  • Pull apart Rump Roast with a serving fork and serve into a bowl with stew covering the meat.

Chicken and Pasta

Prep Time:
 20 Minutes
Total Time:
 1 Hours
Oven Temp:
 350
Servings:
 3-4

Ingredients:

3lbs Chicken Breast 1 Box Rotini 1 Lg Ragu Olive Oil
1 bag cheese mix (optional) Broccoli (optional) 2-3 Bell Pepper (optional) 1-2 cloves Garlic (optional)

Directions:

  • Start by trimming the fat off chicken breasts.
  • Line a baking tray with foil and cooking spray.
  • Salt and Pepper chicken, place in the oven on tray for 30-35 minutes at 350.
  • Fill a 4qt pot 3/4 of the way with cold water.
  • Add a tsp. Salt and a tbsp olive oil to the water. Bring to boil.
  • Once boiling, add Rotini.
  • Prep optional vegetables by steaming broccoli, dicing peppers, or mincing garlic.
  • Strain Pasta once cooked and add to a larger Pan or Wok.
  • Add optional broccoli & peppers to Pan/Wok.
  • Once ready, dice cooked chicken and add to Pan/Wok.
  • Add Ragu to Pan/Wok.
  • Add optional Cheese to Wok. Season with italian seasoning and Garlic Powder to taste. Add optional Garlic.
  • Mix thoroughly. Serve.

Stir Fry

Prep Time:
 20 Minutes
Total Time:
 1 Hours
Oven Temp:
 350
Servings:
 3-4

Ingredients:

3lbs Chicken Breast 1 1/2 Cup Rice Green Beans Broccoli
2-3 Bell Peppers. Soy Sauce. Teriyaki Glaze. Honey.
3 Eggs (optional)

Directions:

  • Start by trimming the fat off chicken breasts.
  • Line a baking tray with foil and cooking spray.
  • Salt and Pepper chicken, place in the oven on tray for 30-35 minutes at 350.
  • Add rice to 2 1/2 cups water.
  • Cook Rice in pressure cooker on high for 4 minutes.
  • Prepare vegetables by dicing peppers and steaming broccoli and green beans.
  • Once ready, dice cooked chicken and add to a large Pan/Wok.
  • Add Rice and vegetables into Pan/Wok
  • Add 1 1/2 tbsp soy sauce.
  • Add 1/2 cup of teriyaki glaze.
  • Add 2 tsp Honey.
  • Carefully crack three eggs in.
  • Mix thoroughly on heat for several minutes to cook eggs.

Pasta Bolegnese

Prep Time:
 5-10 Minutes
Total Time:
 45 Minutes
Oven Temp:
 Stove Top
Servings:
 3-4

Ingredients:

3lb Ground Beef 1 Box Rotini 1 Lg Jar Ragu 2 cloves Garlic
Italian Seasoning Garlic Powder Olive Oil 2-3 Bell Peppers (optional)
Broccoli (optional) Bag of mixed cheese (optional)

Directions:

  • Start by adding oil to Large Pan/Wok and heating.
  • Add Ground beef to Pan and start mixing thoroughly while it cooks.
  • Fill a 4qt Sauce Pot 3/4 with water.
  • Add a tbsp Olive Oil & a tsp Salt to water.
  • Bring water to boil.
  • Steam optional Broccoli and Dice optional Bell peppers.
  • Once Meat is cooked, strain and place back in large pan.
  • Once Pasta is done, strain and place into large pan.
  • Add Lg. Jar Ragu.
  • Add Garlic cloves, optional cheese, optional Bell Peppers, and optional Broccoli and seasonins to taste.
  • Mix thoroughly over medium heat. Serve.

Spaghetti & Meatballs

Prep Time:
 5 Minutes
Total Time:
 25 Minutes
Oven Temp:
 Stove Top
Servings:
 3-4

Ingredients:

1 Box Spaghetti 1 Lg Jar Ragu 1 Box 52ct. Mamma Lucia Meatballs Olive Oil
2 cloves Garlic Italian Seasonig Garlic Powder 1 Bag Mixed Cheese (optional)
1 Box New York Garlic Texas Toast (optional)

Directions:

  • Start by filling a 4qt sauce pan 3/4 with cold water.
  • Add a tsp. Salt and a tbsp olive oil to the water.
  • Bring water to a boil.
  • Steam or Microwave Meatballs.
  • Add in Spaghetti once boiling.
  • Mince garlic cloves.
  • Strain Pasta once cooked.
  • Add Meatballs and spaghetti to larger Pan/Wok when ready.
  • Add Ragu, garlic, optional cheese, and seasonings to taste.
  • Mix thoroughly over medium heat. Serve.
